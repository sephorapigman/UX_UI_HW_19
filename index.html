A solution-savvy UX designer with a track record of success in freelance art, trained at The Ohio State University UX/UI program. As a designer, she led a team to successfully meet branding and technical requirements for various projects including websites/landing pages, logos, and content creation. Strengths lie in defining and executing design strategy built on a deep understanding of both user and business needs to maintain brand loyalty and customer retention. Specializing in user research, usability testing, information architecture, and interaction design, with an aim to create interfaces that create compelling user experiences.

My UX Skills

Empathy & Curiosity

Setting aside assumptions and letting the curiosty of human design take over allows for a user centered design process that highlights their needs

Communication & Collaboration

Communication bridges the gap between the design world and clients. Communication is essential for conveying reasoning and implemented design strategies. A designer should always lean on their teams and stakeholders to ensure everyone is pleased with the progession of the design process. Without collaboration, hours of work could be wasted and stakeholders can lose faith in their design team.

Wireframing & Prototpying

After synthesizing research from interviews and field studies, prototyping begins. Using paper and pen first to get an idea of the IA. After prototyping, wireframing begins, using Figma. Through a series of iterations from either usabilty tests or A/B testing, eventually a High fidelity wirefreame is born and sent off to developers.

My Work

Easterseals Job Board Redesign

People with all abilities struggle to find and attain a job. So for this non-profit, my group and I took on the role to redesign Easterseals' job board to meet accessibility and usabilty standards. We created a responsive webdesign that highlighted the user flow from job search to application.

Peer2Peer Storage Solutions

Peer2Peer is a solution driven app that highlights the monetary inconvience month to month modern day storage has. We were inspired by AirBNB and took on a "host" inspired solution. It marries the world of people with too much space to the people with too many things.
